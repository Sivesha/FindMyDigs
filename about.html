About us

Our Vision

In 2019, safe student accommodation is becoming more critical than ever before. Being away from home for the first time should be an exciting experience where you make friends for life and enjoy the new adventure. In response to the challenges South Africa is facing with safety being in the limelight as a major concern, we have created a platform where you can make an informed decision about where your new home should be. Honest reviews with location from Rhodes University are provided with some additional features that you may find useful. We’re connecting student tenants with landlords from across the country, as well as rental agents with student tenants. Our goal is to build a platform where people can connect with like-minded individuals in a safe, comfortable and secure environment. Take control of your future now and make friends for life.

How does it work?
Students, register on the platform and wait for your request to be approved. Once you have been approved onto the system, to start searching, click on the search bar and type in the name of the Digs. Another option is to simply use the filters provided to get a better idea of what you are looking for. Search and compare properties using the ratings and comments and find the perfect fit for you. Agents or landlords, register on the platform and wait for your request to be approved. Once you have been approved onto the system, click the “Add a digs” button to create a free property listing and fill in the details.
